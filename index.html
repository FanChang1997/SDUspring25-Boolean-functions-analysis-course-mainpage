NKU Probability Seminar (概率论及其相关领域讨论班)

    Welcome! This is a reading group organized by School of Statistics and Data Science, Nankai University . We mainly focus on the following topics:

  • Stochastic analysis and Applications (including Random walks on groups, Non-local operators and Potential Theory, Heat kernel estimate,...)

  • Information theory and Applications (including Noise stability, Isoperimetry and Hypercontractivity, Coupling and Optimal transport,...)

  • Statistical physics (including Bernoulli Percolation,...)

  • Modern discrete probability theory (including Analysis of Boolean functions, Concentration-of-measure inequalities, Markov chains and Mixing times, Random graph theory,...)

  • Deep learning theory
